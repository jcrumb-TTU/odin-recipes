Description

This recipe involves grilling sirloin steak to medium rare/medium and is among the authors favorite dishes to prepare. The recipe is an easy and delicious way to prepare a a sirloin steak that can be served with grilled vegatables, mashed potatoes, etc.

Ingredients

  • Top Sirloin steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Unsalted Butter
