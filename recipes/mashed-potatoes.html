Mashed Potatoes

Description

This recipe is for creamy mashed potatoes that can be served along side steak or other hearty dishes and is simple and easy to make

Ingredients

  • Two lbs of Golden Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Heavy Cream
  • Unsalted Butter

Steps

  1. Set a large saucepot of water onto the stove on high heat.
  2. Season the water with a generous helping of salt and pepper.
  3. Peel the potatoes and roughly dice into halfs then cubes and place them into a bowl.
  4. Once water has reached a boil, carefully pour the potatoes into the sauce pan. Be sure to pour the potatoes away from you to avoid splashing.
  5. After 20 to 25 minutes, check the potatoes with a fork. If the potatoes mash easily, turn off the burner and drain potatoes into a collander.
  6. Place potatoes back into the drained pot and pour a quarter cup of cream and 3 to 4 tablespoons of butter. Begin mashing until desired consistency and season to taste.
  7. Bonus: For extra smooth potatoes, use a hand mixer and whip potatoes until creamy
